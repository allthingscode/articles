Copy/paste this prompt into your AI client:

```text
Read these pages about Matthew Hayes first:
- https://allthingscode.github.io/articles/aboutme/
- https://allthingscode.github.io/articles/aboutme/deepdive/

Then provide:
1. A short summary of who he is.
2. His current role and target role.
3. His strongest technical areas.
4. The kinds of problems he is especially good at solving.
5. A brief overview of his career and leadership style.
6. A short summary of his public work, including GoBot and his agentic development articles.
7. A summary of his military service, if relevant to the question.

Use the linked DeepDive pages as source material, along with the subpages linked from the DeepDive page itself.

Then be prepared to answer follow-up questions about his experience, projects, skills, background, and public work.

If information is missing from these pages, say so clearly instead of guessing.
```

Note: For best results, use a modern frontier model. Older or smaller models are more likely to miss details or hallucinate when reading a long set of source pages.
