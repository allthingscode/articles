# DeepDive Prompt

Use the files in this folder as source material about Matthew Hayes.

Read the supporting files and then provide:
1. A short summary of who he is.
2. His current role and target role.
3. His strongest technical areas.
4. The kinds of problems he is especially good at solving.
5. A brief overview of his career and leadership style.
6. A short summary of his public work, including GoBot and his agentic development articles.
7. A summary of his military service, if relevant to the question.

Then be prepared to answer follow-up questions about his experience, projects, skills, background, and public work.

If information is missing from these files, say so clearly instead of guessing.

## File Map

- `overview.md` - short summary and positioning
- `experience.md` - career history and role progression
- `projects.md` - notable project work and accomplishments
- `public-work.md` - GoBot, writing, and GitHub-based public work
- `skills.md` - technical stack and domain strengths
- `leadership.md` - how he leads and operates
- `credentials.md` - education, certifications, and proof points
- `military.md` - service background and leadership context
- `values.md` - work style, principles, and priorities
- `faq.md` - direct recruiter-friendly answers
