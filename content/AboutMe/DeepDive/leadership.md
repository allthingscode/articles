## Leadership Style
- Hands-on technical leader.
- Architect who builds systems and drives adoption.
- Comfortable operating at the intersection of business priorities and technical execution.
- Prefers practical delivery over abstract planning alone.

## How You Lead
- Translate business priorities into actionable technical designs.
- Break down complex work into clear steps that teams can execute.
- Stay close to implementation so the architecture stays grounded.
- Help teams move faster without sacrificing quality or reliability.

## Scope
- Can lead or contribute at a team size in the 10 to 20 range.
- Comfortable influencing engineering-wide adoption efforts.
- Strong fit for roles where architecture, execution, and rollout all matter.

## What You Are Not
- Not primarily a speaker or presenter.
- Not a pure prompt engineer.
- Not someone who only designs systems from a distance.
- Not someone with formal budget-management ownership.
